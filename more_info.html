Some stuff about me I’m French, which means I criticise cinema like I’m an acclaimed director and I judge food like I’m a certified chef. I grew up in Saint-Denis, a town stuck between Paris and the countryside. Now I live in London, which isn’t much better, cause the cars are on the wrong side and nobody speaks with their hands. When people asked what I wanted to do when I grow up, I always said "draw" to my parents, my teachers, my psychiatrist and my counselors. Some of them believed me and some didn’t, but who’s laughing now ?
